    About Us

    Apart corporation was established in 2017, professional trader for pharmaceutical machineries (local & import), Q.C equipment, Chemical & Reagent, Hospital machinery, Diagnostic Equipment & Reagent, Food Industries, water treatment equipment and automatic packing & intelligent logistic system. We provide all kinds of engineering solution for pharmaceutical, Hospital, Diagnostic, Food Factory in compliance with cGMP / US FDA GMP, WHO GMP, PIC/S GMP principle etc. Our company cooperates closely with well-known project pharmaceuticals industry designing institutes to provide the reasonable project design, Drawing & Commission HVAC system turnkey work, the high quality equipment and the customized service for pharmaceutical & medical turnkey projects. We also provide Mobile Manufacturing Clean Room. We will sincerely cooperate with pharmaceutical and medical enterprises in Bangladesh, grow together and make unremitting efforts for human health.

    *Pre-engineering consulting service. *Production process design. *Concept design and detailed construction design. * Commissioning of HVAC System Turnkey Works for Pharmaceuticals, Hospital & Food Industries * Mobile Manufacturing Clean Room. *Equipment model selection and customization. *Installation and commissioning. *Validation of the equipment and process. *Hard and soft documentation. *Training for skilled workers and so on.

    Our Team

    • As a professional team has more than 15 years experiences and accumulated resources in Pharmaceutical, Hospital & Food industry on turnkey project with very strong technical skills of installation and construction, the projects complied to FAD, GMP, cGMP, WHO GMP ISO9001 and 14000 quality system standards. We ensure products are good quality, Competitive price, high cost effective and profitable. • Our design team led by many senior experts in the Pharmaceutical industry with the outstanding technical ability, skilled in deepening, details strengthening, fully guarantees the effective implementation of the project. We careful calculation, rational planning and cost accounting specialized systematization, scale management and optimize the construction cost of labor, thus ensure the enterprises have a good profit. • With the professional service team support online and offline in multi thus ensure high quality and efficient service.
